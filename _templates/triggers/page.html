My Triggers

Situations, feelings, and environments that cause me to want to drink or experience a craving.

← Back to Recovery Life Skills
These are my own personal triggers — not a general list. What triggers one person may not affect another. I keep this list as a reminder of what I need to watch for and guard against.

Internal Triggers

Emotional and mental states that cause cravings:

  • Boredom
  • Stress
  • Resentments
  • Fears

Environmental Triggers & Rules

Situations and substances to avoid:

  • Never allow alcohol in the house
  • If alcohol is received as a present, do not accept it — give it back
  • No mouthwash (contains alcohol)
  • No food made with alcohol such as tiramisu, beer cheese soup, steak sauces, and so on
  • Avoid addictive medicines
  • Check for alcohol content before taking any OTC or prescription medications
  • Do not attend events where alcohol is served, even family events
  • Leave as soon as people bring out alcohol
  • TV, movies, commercials, and video games can all be triggers
