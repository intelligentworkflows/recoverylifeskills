Upon Awakening

What I do every morning — before anything else.

← Back to Recovery Life Skills
This is my daily morning routine. It is not optional on good days only — the whole point is that it happens on every day, including the ones when it feels least necessary.
If I know I am going to drink today: I go immediately to a meeting and share using the keywords "Burning Desire." I call my sponsor at the same time. I do not wait.
  1. I read pages 87 and 88 I open the Big Book and read the morning prayer on pages 87 and 88. After completing Step 3, I read the Third Step Prayer instead.
  2. I call my sponsor I let them know the answers to four questions:
    • Did we pick up a white chip yesterday?
    • Which meeting are we attending today — or why are we choosing not to attend?
    • What are we doing today to work toward completing our current step?
    • Do we need to be called back, or is a voicemail sufficient?
  3. I attend a meeting with my sponsor twice a week Not every meeting needs to be with my sponsor, but two per week together is my target.

Immediate Amends

When we wake up, we immediately make amends for any harm we caused the day before. This is a powerful way to stay connected to our recovery and to those around us.

Step Work

We work on our current step every day. This helps us to stay focused on our recovery and to continue making progress.
